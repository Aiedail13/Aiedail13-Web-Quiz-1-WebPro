Tourist Spot in Bandung!

Kawah Putih

Kawah Putih is located near the little market town of Ciwidey, approximately 50 Kilometers south of Bandung. As the name suggests, the Crater Lake and its surroundings are dominated by a pale white color which radiates a rather hypnotizing ambience. The vast dormant volcanic crater is filled with surreal turquoise-colored water. The tree-clad cliffs surrounding the crater reach around 2,500 meters above sea level and make for a stunning backdrop, especially when the clouds start to roll in.

Gedung Sate

Gedung Sate Gedung Sate was used as Dutch Indies Government Companies. The roof has a decoration symbolizing 6 million guldens, the cost of the building. The decoration has a shape of popular satay food as now it is famous for the name. Interestingly, this building mixes different architectural styles: Spanish Moorish style for the windows, Italian renaissance for the over all building, and between Balinese pura & Thai's pagoda for the roof.

Braga Street

The Braga Street is located in the heart of Bandung, and it is strategically fitted between Bandung’s finest tourist spots, making it a must visit place that you won’t regret if you have the time. The whole street is the spot, because there is a whole lot of things to enjoy along your course. The atmosphere of this place is a mixture of the complexity of historical significance, the elegance and the trnaquility. The architecture on this street is a mixture of modern building and art-deco style building from the past. Walking in Braga Street feels like taking a step back in time.
